Explanation:

Deliberately telling or showing others their emotions or feelings.

Examples:

  • Saying "I'm tired" or "I'm hungry"
  • Saying "This is boring" or "I don't like this game"
  • Yelling "That's not fair!" during play
  • Showing an adult their scraped knee and looking sad
  • Crossing arms, frowning
  • Crying while looking at an adult for help
  • Hiding behind someone to show fear or discomfort
